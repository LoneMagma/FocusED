Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

The short version

FocusED does not collect, store, or transmit any data — about you, your device, or your usage. There are no servers. There are no analytics. There is no internet permission in the app at all.

Accessibility Service

FocusED uses Android's Accessibility Service to detect which screen is currently open inside Instagram and YouTube. This is the only way to know when Reels or Shorts is active without requiring root access.

The Accessibility Service is used solely to read the name of the currently visible screen. It does not read, record, or transmit the content of any screen, any text you type, any messages, or any other information from any app.

Data collection

None. FocusED has no internet permission (android.permission.INTERNET is not declared in the app manifest). The app is technically incapable of sending any data anywhere.

Third parties

FocusED contains no third-party SDKs, no advertising libraries, no crash reporting tools, and no analytics frameworks.

Children

FocusED does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date at the top.
